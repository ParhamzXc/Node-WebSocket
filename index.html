About Us

A non-profit research organization dedicated to deep-space exploration, the search for extraterrestrial life, and next-generation aerospace propulsion technology.

Our Mission

Cosmic Frontiers was founded in 2030, bringing together the world's leading astrophysicists, engineers, and visionaries.

We believe that through cross-border collaboration and technology sharing, humanity can achieve interstellar migration within half a century.

Our work spans a wide range of fields — from clearing low-Earth orbit debris to constructing deep-space radio telescope arrays. Every small step is a giant leap toward the stars.

Core Research Projects

Discover our ongoing breakthrough projects that are redefining humanity's understanding of the cosmos.

Mars Outpost Alpha

Establishing the first permanent Mars research base to study local geological structures and conduct closed-ecosystem survival experiments.

Deep Space Eye Initiative

Deploying a next-generation Webb-class space telescope array aimed at capturing light from the first 100 million years after the Big Bang.
